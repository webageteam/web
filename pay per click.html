PAY PER CLICK CONSULTING

PPC stands for pay-per-click, a model of internet marketing in which advertisers pay a fee each time one of their ads is clicked. Essentially, it’s a way of buying visits to your site, rather than attempting to “earn” those visits organically.

Pay-per-click ($) = Advertising cost ($) ÷ Ads clicked (#)

What Do Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Consultants Do?
Pay-per-Click (PPC) ad campaign is among the fastest digital avenues to get measurable and scalable results despite the size of a business in terms of resources. In a world where millions to billions of individuals interact with various search engines, PPC campaigns help companies engage whatever size of a defined target and achieve results. However, with PPC consultants, businesses can take advantage of the potential of placing their advertisements in the right user settings. From offering bits of advice to taking the front seat of a Pay-per-Click campaign, these experts possess the knowledge and experience that would help enterprises optimize a PPC focused ad strategy.
